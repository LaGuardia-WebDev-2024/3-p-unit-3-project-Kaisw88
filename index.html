Free For All

Recipe #1

  • Time: 11:14
  • Serves: everybody
Ingredients Quantity
An incredibly tight intro led by Art Blakey 0:50
Wayne Shorters mind boggling tenor solo 3:40
A rhythmic trumpet solo from Freddie Hubbard 2:00
An exciting trombone solo from Curtis Fuller 2:00
An electrifying drum solo from Blakey 1:30

How To Go About This Listening Experience

Step 1: From my expereince, this song has been most thoroughly enjoyed when
walking somewhere in a hurry. If you don't have anywhere to be, perhaps take a
step outside and let the music dictate where you go.
Step 2: I would use your most quality pair of headphones to get the best
experience.
Step 3: Often times, especially when listening to Art Blakey, I will do some air
drumming and act as if I am playing swing on the ride cymbal like Blakey.
